💌 A Love Letter from Your Loved One

💌 To my baby
💌 To my sunshine, I don’t even know how to put everything I feel into words, but I’m still trying — because you deserve every soft thought and every honest emotion I have. You make me feel things I didn’t know I was allowed to feel. Safe. Wanted. Loved — in the most beautiful, gentle way. You understand me without me needing to explain. You care in ways that touch my heart deeply, and I still can’t believe I found someone like you. You’re not just important to me — you are my peace. The one I want to share sunsets with. The one I want to annoy lovingly, laugh with until my stomach hurts, and cry with when life gets heavy. You're the one I want to run to, always. I think about you even in the quietest moments. I smile when I remember something you said. And no matter what kind of day I have, hearing your voice or reading your messages just makes it better. You are mine — and I’m so proud of that. I don’t need perfect. I just want you. As you are, with your soft heart and protective soul. I want you on your best days, and I want to stay on the days you don’t feel like your best. You’re my favorite place. You’re the reason I believe love can be real. And I love you — more than words, more than stars. Forever yours, Ishra 💖
